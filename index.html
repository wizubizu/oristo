ACCELERATOR

Accelerator is the material that when mixed with a catalyst and resin, speeds up the chemical reaction between the catalyst and the resin (usually in the polymerizing of resin).

Read more

POLYESTER-RESIN

Polyester resin are unsaturated synthetic resins formed by the reaction of di-basic organic acids and polyhydric alcohols. They are used in sheets moulding and bulk casting.

Wall panels fabricated from polyester resins are reinforced with fibre glass mats.

Read more

FIRBRE MET

Fibre mat is a common type of fibre reinforced plastic using glass fibre. These fibres may be randomly arranged, flattened into a sheet or woven into a fabric cheaper and more flexible than carbon fibre,

Read more

CATALYST

Catalyst is a component of certain types of mixture. It is simply used to increase the resistance of the mixture once it gets. It some other mixtures a catalyst is used as a curing component.

WOVEN-ROVING

Woven roving is made from continuous glass fiber roving which are interlaced into heavy weight fabrics.

Read more

WAX-88

Wax 88 Meguiar’s Mold Release Wax #88. A blend of imported waxes specially formulated to provide the maximum number of releases per application.

GEL-COAT

Gel-coat or 'GelCoat' is a material used to provide a high-quality finish on the visible surface of a fibre-reinforced composite.

PVA (polyvinyl alcohol)
