ACCELERATOR

Accelerator is the material that when mixed with a catalyst and resin, speeds up the chemical reaction between the catalyst and the resin (usually in the polymerizing of resin). accelerator are also known as promoter when used with polyester resins and vulcanizing agent when used with rubber. The accelerator recommended addition levels are between 0.5%and 2.0%.
